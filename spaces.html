National Autistic Society (UK) - Community

A Community Forum For Individuals with Autism Based in the United Kingdom

About This Organization:

We're here to help the 700,000 autistic people in the UK and their families. Be it running specialist schools, campaigning for improved rights or training companies on being more autism-friendly, we are dedicated to transforming lives and changing attitudes.

Autcraft

A Mincraft Server for People with Autism and their families

About This Organization:

A Minecraft server designed specifically for autistic individuals and their families, providing a welcoming space to socialize and express themselves freely.

r/autism (reddit.com)

A Community Forum For Individuals with Autism on the social networking and forum site Reddit.com

About This Organization:

Autism news, information and support. Please feel free to submit articles to enhance the knowledge, acceptance, understanding and research of Autism and ASD.
